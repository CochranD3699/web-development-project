A little about me

I joined the US Marine Corps shortly after graduating from Sprayberry. Like many in my age group, I didn't really know what I wanted to be when I grew up. Honestly, I think my hope was that I would never have to grow up (or at the very least, not old).

Many, many, many years, a few wrong turns, and maybe a couple of detours later; I realized what was very obvious from my early 20's, I loved working on computers. I love trying to figure out how to get it to do what I want it to. Granted this can sometimes drive me a little crazy when I am right in the middle of a problem. However, the joy and satisfaction of having solved the riddle of the day is incomparable.

I am greatful for more than a few of my detours as that has led me to who I am today. I do not have a prestigous resume, but I do have an awesome family. My husband and I have a wonderful blended family that includes 3 adult children, 2 grandchildren, and 2 cute dogs.

Lastly, I will mention that my husband is a retired Army Officer and I am very much a 1 tour enlisted Marine, but that just goes to show you.
